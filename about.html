About Us

Committed to creating a world where every child thrives with dignity and inclusion

Our Story

Bhartidevi Educational Foundations was established with a vision to create a nurturing environment where disabled and physically challenged children can access quality education, comprehensive care, and specialized therapy services. Founded with deep compassion and commitment, our organization has been serving over 50 children and their families for more than 15 years.

Our journey began when our founder witnessed the challenges faced by children with disabilities in accessing proper education and care. Many families struggled to find inclusive educational institutions and specialized support services. This realization sparked the creation of Bhartidevi Educational Foundations, named in honor of a compassionate soul who believed in the potential of every child.

Today, we stand as a beacon of hope, providing holistic support that addresses the educational, physical, emotional, and social needs of children with disabilities. Our integrated approach combines education, therapy, healthcare, and skill development to empower each child to reach their fullest potential.
